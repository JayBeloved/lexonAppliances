# **App Name**: Lexon Appliance Landing Page

## Core Features:

- Homepage: Landing page with a brief introduction to Lexon Appliance, highlighting its premium quality and affordable prices. It should incorporate the logo provided in the user prompt: https://drive.google.com/open?id=1lJLO2QaycFAr3hNFKGTAM8RK2Dw1qbtu
- About Us: A page detailing the company's mission, vision, and values, emphasizing its commitment to providing high-quality kitchen and home appliances at pocket-friendly prices.
- Product Teaser: A section teasing upcoming products like kettles and toasters, creating anticipation for future releases.
- Contact Form: A simple contact form for inquiries and customer support. Initially store the responses in memory, to be extended with database integration in the future.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) to convey trust and premium quality.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) to provide a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#B8860B) to highlight key information and calls to action, reinforcing the premium aspect.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, providing a modern, yet welcoming feel, that balances the design without overpowering the minimalist visuals
- Clean and minimalist design, drawing inspiration from Binatone's website. Focus on clear information hierarchy and easy navigation.
- Subtle animations to provide feedback and improve user engagement (e.g., form submissions, button presses).
